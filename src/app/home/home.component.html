About this book store

Kenneth E. Hagin was born on the 20th of August, 1917. As a young child, he suffered from a terminal blood disease (deformed heart) that left him bedridden at age 15. As his illness grew worse, his parents, Jess Hagin and Lillie Viola had began making furneral arrangements to bid their son a peaceful farwell. It was in April 1933, he died and was resurrected on three occassions, each time seeing the horrors of hell and then returning to life. This experience was the turning point of his life as he recieved Jesus as his Lord and saviour. Thereafter Jesus completely healed him of the terminal disease with a scripture that eventually birthed the Rhema Bible Church.

History

In 1949, Hagin began an itinerant ministry as a Bible teacher and evangelist. At the same time he recieved full admission into the Full Gospel Business Men's Fellowship International. He was also a part of a major healing revival in U.S that took place in the the 40s and 50s. Kenneth E.
